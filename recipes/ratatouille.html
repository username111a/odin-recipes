Ratatouille

Description

A simple and easy recipe for ratatouille

Ingredients

  • 3 large tomatoes
  • 2 large onions
  • 2 zucchini
  • 2 mushrooms
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic

Steps

  1. Cut up the vegitables in to the sizes of your preference
  2. Heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in a big pot and cook and stir garlic
  3. Add onions and cook until golden brown
  4. Add the rest of the vegetables and cook until done
