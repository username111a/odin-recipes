Dal

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 cup red lentils
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 2 chopped red chiles
  • 2 tablespoon chopped coriander leaves

Steps

  1. Combine the lentils, turmeric, chiles and salt in a pot, add 2 cups of water and bring to a boil
  2. Lower the heat to medium high
  3. Cook for 10-15 minutes
  4. Serve with coriander leaves
